Welcome to the Assemblies of God Campus Ministry! Assemblies of God Campus Ministry (AGCM) is a youthful Ministry from the Missions Department of the Assemblies of God, Ghana. Spanning across several high schools, colleges and univesities, our aim is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ.
Whether you're a devout Christian or just exploring your faith, we invite you to join us for our Bible Studies, Prayer meetings, Retreats and Social Events.
Our Goal is to provide a supportive and welcoming environment where you van grow in your faith and connect with other students.

ABOUT US

Since birth in July 2013, AGCM-UENR has been the mother of over 20 thousand students of UENR. With Mr. Charles as our First President, AGCM has been progressing forward.
Over the years, AGCM-UENR has embarked on numerous Evangeliscal outreaches within the Bono Region, littering the surrounding communities with churches, most of which are standing till date.
AGCM-UENR is currently under the able leadership of MR. Theophilus and under the Fathership of Rev. Joshua Kere

Our Mission

Our Mission is to empower students to know God, grow in their faith, and go into the world to make a difference. We are committed to reaching and transforming the lives of college students and young adults through evangelism, discipleship, leadership development, community building, and mission mobilization. Our goal is to introduce students to a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, deepen their faith, and equip them to become influential leaders in their communities and world changers for Christ. By fostering a sense of belonging and connection among students, facilitating meaningful relationships and support networks, and inspiring them to share the Gospel globally, we aim to make a lasting impact on the next generation of leaders, transforming campuses, communities, and the world for Christ.
